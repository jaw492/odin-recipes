Fruit Cereal

A bowl of fruit cereal

Ingredients

  • nuts
  • chia seeds
  • granola
  • bananas
  • blueberries
  • blackberries
  • cereal

Procedure

  1. Start by washing and chopping your fruit. I love to chop my fruit into different shapes to provide extra texture. For example I may slice half of my banana and chop the other half into smaller pieces.
  2. Once you've prepared all your fruit, place the fruit into a bowl and top it with whatever nuts, seeds, oats, and or dried fruit you desire.
  3. Pour some chilly, non-dairy milk overtop and enjoy!
